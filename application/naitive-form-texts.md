## 2. What are you building / Cosa stai costruendo
*~200 parole*

---

R.O.S.S. is an AI companion for the elderly. Not a medical device — a presence. This distinction is not marketing: it's the foundation of our regulatory strategy, our IP architecture, and every technical decision we've made.

The eldercare market treats elderly people as patients to monitor. Cameras, biometric wearables, SOS buttons. Every product in this space has failed with the exact user we're building for — because she doesn't want to be monitored. She wants to be accompanied.

ROSS runs on a local-first architecture: on-device LLM, speech-to-text and TTS entirely local, zero biometric sensors. The core is a biographical knowledge base — a structured map of the user's people, places, events, and routines — which ROSS uses to hold real conversations, not generic chatbot exchanges. Every day, the family receives a narrative letter about her day. Not a dashboard. A letter.

We're 4 co-founders. I run finance and commercial development: TAM Italy ~€3.8B, SAM ~€941M, pricing at €1,000 + €40/month — less than one month of a live-in carer. We were selected as top project at H-HACK in April 2026 and are now building inside H-Farm's Venture Building Program, with two patent filings in progress.

ROSS is not a project. It's a company being built.
